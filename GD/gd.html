Graphic Designe

I am a passionate and creative Graphic Designer with a strong eye for visual aesthetics and detail. My expertise lies in creating visually compelling designs that effectively communicate messages and engage audiences. I specialize in logo design, branding, UI/UX design, and digital illustrations, combining artistic creativity with technical precision to produce high-quality graphics.

I have gained my experience and developed my portfolio during my time at TUMO Tirana, where I completed three intensive workshops focused on graphic design. Through these workshops, I honed my skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma, mastering concepts such as color theory, typography, and composition to create professional and impactful designs.

Beyond aesthetics, I am dedicated to understanding user experience and brand identity, ensuring that my designs align with the client's vision and objectives. I am always eager to explore new design trends, techniques, and tools to enhance my creativity and stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of graphic design.
